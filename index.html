* Interesting facts about dreamcore

* Key Features of Dreamcore
* Dreamcore is an online aesthetic that uses surreal imagery to evoke the sensation of being in a dream, evoking feelings of déjà vu, nostalgia, or mild anxiety. Its key elements are:
* Not to Scare: Unlike the similar Weirdcore aesthetic, which focuses on nightmares and fear, Dreamcore strives to convey the full range of feelings found in dreams—from strange comfort to mystery.
* Distinctive Visuals: Images and videos often feature:
* Liminal Spaces: Empty transitional zones (hallways, foggy parks) that feel familiar yet unreal.
* Bright or Pastel Colors: The palette can range from light and soft to starkly bright.
* Surreal Elements: Clouds, eyes, portals, doors, floating buildings, and unnatural-looking characters.
* Simple Text: Short phrases or monologues, often set in Arial or Times New Roman fonts.
* Connections to Games and Childhood: The aesthetic often draws inspiration from childhood memories and video games. Soundtracks from Earthbound, Yume Nikki, and Minecraft are considered part of its cultural code.
